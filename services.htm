We understand the concepts and the tricky situation of this issue and our dedicated and specialized teams are able to rapidly source any shortage, obsolete, or EOL electronic components from around the world. Our Sales team handling and covering the globe will dig inch by inch their territories through their respective designated geographical areas to find that right electronic components that you need for your production line, so you can sleep peace of mind once your requirements submitted to us, and be assured that once you wake up the day after CompoHub will deliver.

Excess Material Management:
We know that our customers face some issues as over-forecasting, technology change, PCB upgrades, cancellation and end of life situations all this can result on excess inventories, this surplus inventory ties up capital, manpower, and warehousing space.
Our excess management team is dedicated and specialized in only proactively get you the best solution to maximize returns on your excess inventory, with effective and confidential ways.
